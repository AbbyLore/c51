Panda

Such a cute panda! Here are some facts.

  • Pandas have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
  • Like domestic cats, giant pandas have vertical slits for pupils.
  • Sometimes, to mark their scent, panda's climb a tree backwards with their hindfeet until they're in a full handstand upside down - enabling them to leave their scent higher up.

Pigeon

From my experience, pigeons love banana bread! Go, Banana Bread Batallion!

  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals. They will often be seen in flocks of 20-30 birds.
  • Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities. They can detect sounds at far lower frequencies than humans are able to, and can thus hear distant storms and volcanoes.

Bear

I hope you could... BEAR with me through these facts! Pun intended.

  • Grizzly bears can remember the faces of other bears.
  • The Governor of Moscow trained a large bear to serve pepper Vodka to his guests.
  • Unlike many mammals, bears can see in color.
